
# EcoSort
## Problem
According to EPA's article about composting they state that in 2019, **5% of the 66.2 million tons** of wasted food generated by food retail was composted. In addition, to the incredibly low compost rate, Keefe Harrison, the CFO of The Recycling Partnership said the following, "Every year we trash **79%** of recyclables but we don’t need to." 
## Solution
This is where an idea was born, **EcoSort**. EcoSort is an automated sorting system for your trash. EcoSort will use a camera in parallel with a **CNN** AI model to classify items into trash, compost, or recycling bins. EcoSort comes with one goal being to make the Earth more sustainable.
## Process
**1.** Place item on the platform.
**2.** Camera takes photo of item and runs it through the **CNN** model.
**3.** Moves the platform with the item on it to the right bin.
**4.** Opens the platform opening to drop the item into the bin.
**5.** Then the platform moves back to it's starting position ready to start again.
